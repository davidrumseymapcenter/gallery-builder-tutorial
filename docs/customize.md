# Customize your view

The interface offers a few ways to change how the gallery appears in a browser window.
<hr>

## Rearrange your collection

- You can reorder cards as needed by dragging and dropping. 

- Click within the card outline but outside of the thumbnail image to drag a card.

- If you want to remove a card, click the x in the upper right corner.
<hr>

## Change what you can see

- Use the control links at the upper right of the input panel to hide and show the inputs and hide and show the viewing pane. 

- When you're not actively adding content it can be nice to hide the input panels and get a better look at the gallery grid.

- Change the size of the viewing pane by dragging the vertical grey bar left or right to your desired location.
